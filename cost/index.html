The Cost of UNL

The cost of attending the University of Nerbaska-Lincoln rose steadily until 1999...

The cost for both in-state and out-of-state students skyrocketed. Ironically, while the tuition increased graduation rates at UNL remained the lowest in the Big Ten. At almost $22,000 for in-state students and $34,500 for out-of-state students, UNL's cost of attendance is the lowest in the Big Ten as well. Northwestern University, the most expensive school in the conference, has the highest graduation rate at 93% for a universal $60,840 price tag. Compared to other Nebraska Universities, UNL has the lowest percentage of students recieveing Pell grants and higher cost for low-income students. UNL students on Pell Grant scholarships do a better job of paying off their grants though. 18.3% of University of Nebraska at Omaha students don't payback their grants while only 12.2% of UNL Pell Grant recipients don't pay back their grants.
