about me

I’m 19 years old and I split my time between Kansas City, San Francisco and Lincoln. I’m a sucker for a good playlist and I always end up throwing Earl Sweatshirt on the same playlist as Tame Impala, The Kooks, Al Green and Mac Demarco. I love to dance, especially outside of the ballet studio. I’m lucky enough to leave the Midwest every summer and trade the fields for waves. I’m pathetically in love with the ocean and any opportunity I can get to head out there is one I can’t turn down. Here are a few more facts so you can get to know me a little better:

  1. My last name is pronounced "lucky" (I promise)
  2. I’m obsessed with tennis shoes and baseball caps
  3. When I was little I had my heart set on being an archeologist
  4. Stellar is one of my favorite words
  5. My proudest playlist is 728 songs long and isn’t going to slow down anytime soon
  6. My best friend got me hooked on cuties, I swear I eat at least 3 of them a day
  7. I’m convinced "Going to California" by Led Zeppelin is one of the most beautiful songs ever
  8. I dance all the time, especially when I’m driving
  9. Speaking of driving, it’s my favorite way to clear my head. Long drives and good music are the cure to almost anything
  10. My number one motivation at the moment is to get a dog named Marv (after the legendary original baby-making music maker, Marvin Gaye) and I have a collection of pup photos for potential candidates
